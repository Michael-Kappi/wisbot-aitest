# WisBot Platform - Frequently Asked Questions

## General

### Q: What is WisBot?
**A:** WisBot is an enterprise platform for managing truck configurations, weight calculations, and compliance validation. It helps fleet operators ensure their vehicles meet regulatory requirements.

### Q: What browsers are supported?
**A:** WisBot supports the latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and Safari. Internet Explorer is not supported.

### Q: Can I use WisBot on mobile devices?
**A:** WisBot is optimized for desktop use. While the interface is responsive, the configuration tools work best on screens 1024px or wider.

## Configuration

### Q: How do I select different tyres for the truck and trailer?
**A:** In the Configuration screen, scroll to the Tyres section. You'll see separate dropdowns for Truck Tyres and Trailer Tyres. Each axle position has its own tyre selector, so you can mix and match tyre types as needed.

### Q: Can I save multiple configurations?
**A:** Yes. Click "Save As" to create a new saved configuration. You can have unlimited saved configurations. Use meaningful names to find them easily later.

### Q: How do I compare two configurations?
**A:** Open the first configuration, then click "Compare" in the toolbar. Select the second configuration from the list. The system shows a side-by-side comparison highlighting differences.

## Weight Calculator

### Q: Why does my total weight not match my manual calculation?
**A:** The Weight Calculator includes all components: chassis, cab, axles, tyres, fuel, fluids, spare wheel, and standard equipment. Your manual calculation may be missing some of these items. Check the detailed weight breakdown for a full list.

### Q: What happens if I exceed the weight limit?
**A:** The Validation Engine will show a red alert. You cannot submit a configuration that exceeds regulatory weight limits. You'll need to reduce cargo weight or change the vehicle configuration to comply.

### Q: How accurate are the weight calculations?
**A:** Weight data comes from manufacturer specifications and is accurate to within 1-2%. Actual weights may vary slightly due to manufacturing tolerances and optional equipment.

## Validation

### Q: Which regulations does the validation check?
**A:** The system checks against EU Directive 96/53/EC for European operations, and can be configured for country-specific regulations. Contact your administrator to set the applicable regulation set.

### Q: Can I override a validation warning?
**A:** Yellow warnings can be acknowledged and overridden with a reason. Red alerts (limit exceeded) cannot be overridden and must be resolved before saving.

### Q: How often are regulation databases updated?
**A:** Regulation databases are updated quarterly. Your administrator receives notifications when updates are available.

## Troubleshooting

### Q: The application is running slowly. What can I do?
**A:** Try clearing your browser cache, closing unused tabs, and ensuring you have a stable network connection. If the problem persists, contact your system administrator.

### Q: I can't log in. What should I do?
**A:** Ensure you're using your enterprise credentials (not a personal account). If your password has expired, reset it through your company's identity portal. Contact IT support if the problem continues.

### Q: My saved configuration is missing. Where did it go?
**A:** Configurations are stored in your user profile. Check that you're logged in with the correct account. If you recently changed departments, your configurations may have been migrated. Contact your administrator for help.
